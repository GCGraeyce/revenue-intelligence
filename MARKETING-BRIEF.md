# RevOS Marketing Brief

**Purpose:** Website copy, positioning, and content strategy for revos.ai (or equivalent domain)
**Product:** RevOS — AI-Powered Revenue Intelligence OS for Zoho CRM
**Target Market:** B2B sales teams (10-100 reps) using Zoho CRM

## 2. Target Personas & Messaging

### Persona 1: VP Sales / CRO

**Their pain:** "I don't trust the forecast. Reps sandbagging. Managers optimising. I find out we're going to miss 2 weeks before quarter-end."

**Our message:** RevOS gives you a quality-adjusted forecast — not rep opinions. Every deal has a Pipeline Quality Score built from engagement signals, stakeholder mapping, and process compliance. You see the real number, with confidence bands, updated daily.

**Hero stat:** "Forecast confidence bands narrow by 40% within 90 days as deal quality data compounds."

**Key features for this persona:**
- Quality-adjusted pipeline with low/mid/high confidence bands
- Risk exposure dashboard (total ACV at risk, by cause)
- Segment breakdown (SMB/Mid-Market/Enterprise pipeline health)
- Executive forecast summary (one-click meeting prep)
- Grade distribution (what % of your pipeline is actually closeable)

**CTA:** "See your real forecast"

---

**CTA:** "Coach smarter, not harder"

## 3. Feature Map (Website Sections)

### Section 1: Pipeline Quality Score (PQS)

**Headline:** "Not all pipeline is created equal."

**Copy:** Your CRM shows you €10M in pipeline. But how much of it is real? RevOS scores every deal across 7 dimensions — engagement velocity, stakeholder coverage, process adherence, ICP fit, stage progression, price risk, and outcome probability — and gives it a grade from A to F.

**Visual:** Score radar chart showing the 7 dimensions for a sample deal, with grade badge (B, 72).

**Proof points:**
- 7 scoring dimensions, weighted and configurable
- Scores update in real-time as CRM signals arrive
- Grades: A (80+), B (65+), C (50+), D (35+), F (<35)
- Self-learning model retrains on your closed-deal outcomes

---

### Section 2: AI Coaching Engine

**Headline:** "Your best manager, for every rep, on every deal."

**Copy:** RevOS doesn't just flag problems — it fixes them. Four executable coaching plays guide your team through the exact steps to recover stalled deals, engage missing buyers, multi-thread enterprise accounts, and handle competitive objections.

**Visual:** Coaching play card (EB Recovery) showing 6 steps, progress bar, estimated time, and expected PQS impact.

**Plays to highlight:**
| Play | Trigger | Expected Impact |
|------|---------|-----------------|
| EB Recovery | Proposal-stage deal, no Economic Buyer | +15-25 PQS points |
| Stall Breaker | Deal stuck 20+ days | Unstall or disqualify in 2 weeks |
| Multi-Threading | Enterprise deal, 1-2 contacts | 2+ new personas engaged |
| Objection Resolution | Competitor mentioned + PQS < 55 | Handle objection with battlecard |

**Plus:** 59 AI coaching prompts across 11 funnel stages — from deal strategy to QBR prep.

---

### Section 3: AI Copilot

**Headline:** "Ask your pipeline anything."

**Copy:** A context-aware AI assistant that lives in every page. Ask "Which deals are most at risk?" and get an instant answer with live data, risk factors, and one-click CRM actions. Ask "Prep my 1:1 with Sophie" and get a full coaching agenda in seconds.

**Visual:** Chat overlay showing a query and rich response with CRM action buttons.

**Capabilities:**
- Natural language pipeline queries
- Prompt-library-powered responses (forecast summaries, risk reports, 1:1 prep)
- One-click CRM write-back (push notes, create tasks, trigger coaching plays)
- Context-aware suggestions based on current page and role

---

### Section 4: Forecast Intelligence

**Headline:** "The forecast your board can trust."

**Copy:** Raw pipeline totals lie. RevOS applies quality-adjusted weighting to show you three numbers: the worst case, the likely case, and the stretch case — each backed by deal-level scoring and historical conversion rates.

**Visual:** Forecast band showing raw (€10.2M) vs quality-adjusted (€7.1M) with confidence range.

**Key points:**
- Quality-adjusted pipeline = ACV x win probability (derived from PQS + historical data)
- Confidence bands (low/mid/high) based on deal grade distribution
- Commit/Best Case/Pipeline category breakdown
- Slip risk exposure (deals likely to push to next quarter)

---

### Section 5: Meeting Prep

**Headline:** "Prep any meeting in 30 seconds."

**Copy:** Forecast call with the CRO? Pipeline review with the team? 1:1 with your struggling rep? QBR with the board? RevOS generates data-driven agendas for each, powered by your live pipeline.

**Templates:**
1. **Forecast Call Prep** — Commit/Best Case breakdown, risk flags, gap analysis
2. **Pipeline Risk Review** — At-risk deals, stalled opportunities, intervention priorities
3. **1:1 Coaching Prep** — Rep performance snapshot, talking points, coaching recommendations
4. **QBR Outline** — Quarterly performance, pipeline health, competitive landscape, next-quarter priorities

---

### Section 6: Zoho CRM Integration

**Headline:** "Lives inside your CRM. Not beside it."

**Copy:** RevOS connects to Zoho CRM with OAuth2 — no data migration, no CSV imports, no dual entry. Your deals, contacts, activities, and notes sync bi-directionally. AI-generated coaching actions write back to Zoho as tasks and notes.

**Integration points:**
- Bi-directional deal sync (read deals, write PQS scores back)
- Contact and account mapping (buying committee → persona coverage)
- Activity ingestion (emails, calls, meetings → engagement scoring)
- Write-back: AI-generated notes, tasks, forecast adjustments
- Batch operations (sync all PQS scores in one click)

**Trust line:** "Your data stays in your Zoho instance. RevOS reads and writes through your OAuth connection."

---

### Section 7: Risk Intelligence

**Headline:** "See the risks your team can't."

**Copy:** RevOS clusters your at-risk pipeline into actionable groups — single-stakeholder enterprise deals, stalled post-proposal opportunities, low-fit outbound prospects, and discount risk deals. Each cluster comes with a playbook to fix it.

**Risk clusters:**
| Cluster | Description | Typical Action |
|---------|-------------|----------------|
| Single-Stakeholder Enterprise | Large deals with only 1 buyer contact | Multi-threading campaign |
| Stalled After Proposal | 20+ days waiting post-proposal | Stall Breaker play |
| Low-Fit Outbound | Cold outreach to poor-fit companies | Disqualify or reposition |
| Discount Risk | 70%+ chance of price negotiation | ROI case + value selling |

**Plus:** Competitive intelligence with win rates, battlecards, and pricing benchmarks for 5+ competitors.

## 8. Brand Voice

**Tone:** Confident, direct, data-literate. Speaks like a sales leader who also understands AI — not a tech vendor trying to sell to sales.

**Avoid:** "Leverage AI to synergise your pipeline transformation." (Corporate fluff)
**Use:** "Your B-grade deals are your biggest opportunity. Here's how to move 10 of them to A." (Specific, actionable)

**Voice characteristics:**
- **Numbers over adjectives** — "7-dimension scoring" not "comprehensive scoring"
- **Actions over insights** — "Creates a CRM task" not "Provides actionable insights"
- **Roles over users** — "Your manager sees the team view" not "Users can access different dashboards"
- **Outcomes over features** — "Trust the forecast" not "Quality-adjusted pipeline analytics"

## 9. Visual Identity Notes

The product uses a dark-mode design system with:
- **Glass card components** (subtle borders, hover elevation)
- **Grade colours:** Green (A) → Teal (B) → Amber (C) → Orange (D) → Red (F)
- **Monospace data typography** (JetBrains Mono for all metrics)
- **Clean data density** (lots of information, no clutter)

The website should reflect this: dark hero sections, live data visualisations, grade colour accents. The product screenshots ARE the marketing — the UI is designed to look impressive.
